ABOUT US

We're a group of queer Montrealers building community care structures in the face of the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic (and helping people understand how bad it is out there. Did you know? It's bad out there!).

Initially part of Mask4Mask-MTL, a Discord server which aims to connect the queer COVID-aware community of Montreal, our group grew out of a desire to dedicate more energy to activism and advocacy around COVID awareness and safety.

> If this sounds like your jam, give us a shout at covidactionnow@proton.me!

We will be updating this page with links to resources, including places to buy PPE, instructions on setting up buyer's clubs, and information on COVID-safe events taking place in Montréal.

If you're looking for information, HEAL XO created this incredible list of COVID-19 resources and research. It's got tons of links, and it's beautifully organized. Check it out!
